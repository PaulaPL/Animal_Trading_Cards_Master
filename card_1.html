Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is instinctively fond of swimming, owing to its history as a game retriever.

  • Origin: Scotland
  • Coat: straight or moderately wavy
  • Color: any shades of gold or cream
  • Life span: average 10-12 years

The temperament of the Golden Retriever is a hallmark of the breed, and is described in the standard as "kindly, friendly and confident". Golden Retrievers make good family pets, particularly as they are patient with children. They are not "one-man dogs" and are generally equally amiable with both strangers and those familiar to them.
